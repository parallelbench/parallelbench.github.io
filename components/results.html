Results on ParallelBench

Evaluation of LLaDA 1.5 across all 17 tasks reveals systematic quality degradation as parallelism increases.

Waiting Line

Key Insight

Replace Index and Replace Random exhibit contrasting trends. Despite Replace Random achieving near-perfect one-by-one decoding accuracy compared to Replace Index's below 50% accuracy, this advantage reverses under parallelization. This aligns with our theory: C(Y|X)=0 for Replace Index but C(Y|X)>0 for Replace Random.

Text Writing

Puzzles

Key Insight

Latin Square outperforms the harder Sudoku under one-by-one decoding, but this advantage disappears with increased parallelism. This reflects the difference in conditional total correlation: C(Y|X)>0 for Latin Square, while C(Y|X)=0 for Sudoku.
