Results on ParallelBench

Evaluation of LLaDA 1.5 across all 17 tasks reveals systematic quality degradation as parallelism increases.

Waiting Line

Key Insight

Tasks with strong token dependencies (Shuffle, Replace Random) show the most dramatic accuracy drops, while order-independent tasks (Copy) maintain near-perfect accuracy even at high parallelism levels.

Text Writing

Puzzles

Key Insight

Sudoku (unique solution) and Latin Square (multiple solutions) reveal distinct failure modes: parallel decoding struggles more when the solution space is highly constrained, highlighting fundamental limitations in constraint satisfaction.
